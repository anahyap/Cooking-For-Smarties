Beginner's Equipment Guide

Chefs Knife & Paring Knife
It's essential to have a good knife that can cut all of your meats, fruits, and veggies. I prefer to have one thats big and a smaller one for precision. If you'd like to see how to use your knife to chop certain vegetables, you can check beginner tutorials on Bon Appétit.

Cutting Board
You'll need a cutting board to pair with the knives. There are many different ones including wooden, plastic, and glass. I would recommend glass to avoid small wooden and plastic particles from getting into your food.

Mixing/Seasoning Bowls
This bowl is essential when needing a vessel to season your food in, toss in sauce, or to marinate .

Measuring Cups/Spoons
When following recipes, havinf an exact measurement helps to accurately follow the recipe. Once you're a bit more experienced, you'll probably need these less and less.

12inch Skillet
This pan is great for cooking your meats in and/or sautéing vegetables.

3qt Saucepan With Lid
Use this bigger pot for pastas and sauces.

Cast Iron Skillet
Cast iron skillets can be used for getting great getting great sears on your meats and great to bake in as well.

Sheet Pan
A sheet pan is great for all things baking! If you want inspiration for sheet pan meals, you can browse ideas on Tasty.

Spatula, Tongs, & Wooden Spoon
These are just the basic essentials to help you turn, stir, and flip your food while its raw and while cooking.

Basic Seasonings & Oils
Black Pepper, Salt, Roasted Garlic Powder, Onion Powder, Chicken Bouillon, Red Cayenne Powder, Chili Powder, Smoked Paprika, Italian Blend, Olive oil, Avocado oil, and Vegetable oil.
