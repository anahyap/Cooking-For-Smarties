Smothered Chicken Over Rice



Wow your guests with this super easy and beginner friendly recipe including tasty chicken thighs, onions, peppers, and gravy over top of your choice of rice or mashed potatoes.


Ingredients:

  • 4 Chicken Thighs
  • 2 tablespoons of Olive Oil
  • 1 teaspoon of all listed seasonings: Black Pepper, Salt, Roasted Garlic Powder, Onion Powder, Italian Seasoning Blend, Chicken Bouillon, Smoked Paprika, Cajun Seasoning Blend
  • 1/4 cups of Flour
  • 1/2 stick of Butter
  • 1/2 chopped White Onion
  • 1 Green Bell Pepper

    • Directions:

    1. Heat oven to 400°F. Line a sheet pan; lightly oil.
    2. Season chicken: toss with avocado oil and all seasonings listed.
    3. Over medium heat, add avocado oil to the pan, sear your chicken on both sides until it has a nice brown coloring.
    4. Remove the chicken from the pan into a baking dish and add your chopped onions and bell peppers.
    5. Place into the oven covered with foil for 45 minutes. Allow to continue baking for 10 minutes uncovered.
    6. In the same pan you seared your chicken with, all in your butter. Allow it to melt, then begin to whisk in your flour. After about a minute or so, add in any additional liquid thar collected in the bottom of your baking dish from your chicken and add it into the mixture, then add about a cup of water. Season generously, and allow to simmer and thicken.
    7. Serve chicken thighs with onions and peppers over a bed of rice with gravy drizzled on top. Enjoy!

    10-Minute Garlic-Butter Lemon Shrimp



    Leave your taste beds satisfied with this quick and easy shrimp scampi inspired recipe thats delicious and can be paired with rice, pasta, or mashed potatoes!


    Ingredients:

    • 1 lb Shrimp, peeled and deveined, and patted dry
    • 2 tbsp butter
    • 4 garlic cloves, minced
    • 1 lemon, zest + 2 tbsp juice
    • 1/2 tsp paprika, pinch red pepper flakes, 3/4 tsp kosher salt, 1/4 tsp black pepper, Chopped parsley

    Directions:

    1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high; add butter.
    2. Add shrimp, salt, pepper, paprika. Cook 1 to 2 minutes until just pink on one side
    3. Flip; add garlic and flakes. Cook 1 to 2 minutes more.
    4. Turn off your heat; add lemon zest & juice, toss. Sprinkle parsley. Serve.

    Quick Garlic Butter Steak Bites



    Juicy, seared beef cubes in a garlicky butter sauce thats ready in 15 minutes with pantry staples.

    Ingredients

    • 1 lb sirloin or ribeye, cut into 1-inch cubes
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 tbsp butter
    • 3 garlic cloves, minced
    • 1 tsp steak seasoning or salt & pepper
    • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce (optional)
    • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

    Directions

    1. Pat steak dry, season well.
    2. Heat oil in a hot skillet; sear steak bites 1–2 minutes per side until browned. Remove.
    3. Add butter and garlic to pan; cook 30 seconds.
    4. Return steak, splash Worcestershire, toss to coat. Sprinkle parsley and serve.

    Honey-Lemon Baked Salmon



    Sweet, tangy, and tender salmon fillets with minimal prep! Perfect for weeknights.

    Ingredients

    • 2 salmon fillets (about 6 oz each)
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 tbsp honey
    • 1 tbsp lemon juice + lemon slices
    • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
    • Salt & pepper
    • Fresh parsley or dill (optional)

    Directions

    1. Heat oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a small pan with foil.
    2. Place salmon skin-side down; whisk oil, honey, lemon, garlic, salt & pepper. Brush over salmon.
    3. Top with lemon slices; bake 10–12 minutes (or until flakes easily, ~125–135°F).
    4. Rest 2 minutes; garnish with herbs and serve.
